LSX Sand Washing Machine

SBM LSX series screw sand washing machine is designed to bigger capacity and higher cleaning level than traditional washing machines. It sets three functions—washing, dewatering, classifying—as a whole, saving the labor and working site space. Now this series sand washing machine has been widely used in various industries.

Features

  • Long service life and no quick-wear part, leading to low investment cost and operation cost
  • High capacity and less loss of fine products
  • Transmission bearings of impeller depart from water and material, avoiding being eroded.
