Raymond Mill

The Raymond grinding mill was ever popular in mining, building materials, chemical engineering and metallurgy industries for size reduction, dispersion, or specialized needs, powder generation and processing for industrial process. Now MTM series grinding mill has better performance than it.

Features

  • Take little working spaces
  • Closed system and steady performance
  • Durable and long service life
  • It can be controlled intensively and high automation

Application

Raymond mill or Raymond grinder is widely used to grind non-flammable and non-explosive materials like barite, calcite, potash feldspar, dolomite, gypsum, talcum, mica, marble, limestone, kaolin, clay, coal etc. It is ever the mainstream grinding machine in powder making industry.
